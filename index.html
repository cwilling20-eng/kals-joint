Our Story

Two Cuisines, One Kitchen

At Kal's Joint, Mediterranean tradition meets Italian comfort in every dish. Our family brings together generations of Lebanese cooking with the bold, familiar flavors of Italian cuisine — creating something you won't find anywhere else in the Webster and Clear Lake area.

Every meal starts with fresh ingredients, prepared from scratch in our kitchen. From hand-rolled gyro wraps dressed with homemade tzatziki to creamy fettuccine alfredo finished with garlic toast, we put the same care into every plate that we'd serve at our own family table.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch during the work day, feeding the family after a Saturday game, or picking up takeout on your way home from NASA, Kal's Joint is your neighborhood spot for something fresh, filling, and a little different.
