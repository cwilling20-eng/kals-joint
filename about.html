About Kal's Joint

A Family Kitchen in the Heart of Webster

Kal's Joint started with a simple idea: bring together the bold, aromatic flavors of Lebanese cooking and the hearty, comforting dishes of Italian cuisine — all under one roof. We're a family-owned restaurant built on recipes passed through generations, reimagined as something entirely our own. From gyro wraps dressed with homemade tzatziki to creamy fettuccine alfredo finished with garlic toast, every dish on our menu reflects the two culinary traditions our family grew up on.

Located at 13914 State Hwy 3, Suite 600 in Webster, TX, we're proud to serve the Clear Lake, League City, Friendswood, Nassau Bay, Seabrook, Kemah, and greater NASA area. We've only been open for a year, but Kal's Joint has already become a go-to spot for locals looking for something different — Mediterranean flavors and Italian comfort food made fresh, every single day.

Everything at Kal's Joint is made from scratch in our kitchen. There are no freezer meals, no shortcuts, and no cutting corners. We source fresh ingredients, prepare our sauces and dressings in house, and hand-roll our wraps to order. The portions are generous because we believe a meal should leave you full and satisfied — the same way we'd feed our own family.

Whether you're a NASA employee grabbing lunch, a family picking up dinner after a long day, or a local professional looking for your new favorite spot, Kal's Joint is here to feed you well. We're not just a restaurant — we're your neighbors, and we take pride in being part of this community.
